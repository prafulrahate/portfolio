Introduction

In today's data-driven world, the role of a data analyst has become increasingly vital across industries. From finance to healthcare, retail to technology, data analysts play a crucial role in uncovering valuable insights from vast amounts of information. In this page, you will explore the projects I have done and the profound impacts they can have on businesses and society as a whole.

Why Projects?

Projects serve as tangible evidence of an analyst's capabilities. Creating a portfolio of projects demonstrates one's ability to handle different types of data and solve analytical challenges. Undertaking projects as a data analyst is essential for practical skill development, building a portfolio, problem-solving, effective communication, domain knowledge acquisition, networking, and continuous learning. Projects not only enhance an analyst's expertise but also provide a strong foundation for a successful career in the data analysis field.
